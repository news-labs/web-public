## Mission

Newsfork is the world's government news intelligence layer, powered by AI. We help developers, analysts, and organisations access structured government news data from 100+ countries through a unified API.

## What we build

- **Newsfork API** — REST API for government news discovery, search, and metadata
- **News-V scoring** — Proprietary relevance and quality scoring
- **CuFork Ads** — Advertising platform for diaspora news publishers
