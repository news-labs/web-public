## Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") supplements our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms/). Violations may result in suspension or termination of API access.

## Prohibited activities

You may not use Newsfork to:

- Violate applicable laws or third-party rights
- Scrape, mirror, or bulk-download the full Newsfork dataset
- Resell raw API access without a written partnership agreement
- Circumvent rate limits, authentication, or access controls
- Transmit malware, spam, or abusive automated traffic
- Misrepresent the source or accuracy of government news data

## Attribution

Applications that display Newsfork-sourced content must clearly attribute Newsfork and, where applicable, the original publisher.

## Security

You must protect API keys, rotate compromised credentials promptly, and report suspected abuse to [hello@newsfork.com](mailto:hello@newsfork.com).

## Enforcement

We may investigate suspected violations, throttle or suspend access, and cooperate with law enforcement when required.

Questions about acceptable use? Email [hello@newsfork.com](mailto:hello@newsfork.com).
