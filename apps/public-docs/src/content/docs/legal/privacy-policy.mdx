## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how [LEGAL_ENTITY_NAME] ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data in connection with the Newsfork platform and related services.

This policy applies to:

- visitors to `www.newsfork.com` and `docs.newsfork.com`;

- any other individuals whose personal data we process.

For personal data we process on behalf of customers (as a processor/data intermediary), the customer's privacy policy governs, and our processing is subject to our [Data Processing Agreement](/legal/data-processing-agreement/).

## 2. Data Controller and Processor Roles

**As Data Controller:** When we collect personal data directly from you (account registration, website visits, marketing communications), we act as the data controller.

**As Processor / Data Intermediary:** When customers upload personal data to the platform, we process such data on behalf of and under the instructions of our customers.

## 3. Personal Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account and Registration Information

- Full name
- Email address
- Company name and job title
- Country/region
- Login credentials (passwords stored in hashed form)

### 3.2 Usage and Technical Information

- IP address and device identifiers
- Browser type and version
- API usage logs (endpoints, timestamps, response codes)
- Pages visited and features used
- Referral URLs and session data

### 3.3 Customer-Uploaded Data

Customers may upload business data that includes personal data (e.g., contact records in integrated applications). We process this data solely as instructed by the customer.

### 3.4 Communications Data

- Support and sales correspondence
- Feedback and survey responses
- Marketing preferences

### 3.5 Payment Information

- Billing address
- Payment card details (processed by PCI-compliant payment processors; we do not store full card numbers)

## 5. Consent

By providing personal data in the course of using our services, you are deemed to consent to collection and use for the purposes described here, where permitted by law.

For marketing communications, we obtain express consent where required.

You may withdraw consent by contacting [privacy@newsfork.com](mailto:privacy@newsfork.com). Withdrawal is prospective and may affect your ability to use certain features.

## 6. Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose personal data to:

- **Service providers** (Cloudflare, Stripe, email delivery, analytics) under data protection agreements
- **Professional advisors** where necessary
- **Regulatory authorities** when required by law
- **Acquirers** in connection with a merger or asset sale (with notice)

We do not sell personal data. A sub-processor list is available upon request.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Personal data may be stored or processed outside your jurisdiction, including the United States, European Union, and Asia-Pacific.

We implement appropriate safeguards, including contractual clauses and selection of providers with recognized security certifications.

For GDPR-subject data, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved transfer mechanisms where required.

## 8. Data Retention

| Data Category | Retention Period |
| --- | --- |
| Account information | Duration of account + 3 years after closure |
| Customer-uploaded data | Duration of subscription + 30 days (then deleted per DPA) |
| API usage logs | 90 days (rolling) |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal/tax requirements) |
| Support correspondence | 3 years after last contact |

After the applicable period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 9. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
- Role-based access and MFA for employees
- Cloud infrastructure with SOC 2 / ISO 27001 certifications
- Monitoring, logging, and incident response procedures

No system is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a breach affecting personal data, we will investigate, contain, and notify affected individuals and regulators where required by law.

For Customer Data, we notify the customer in accordance with our DPA so the customer can fulfill its own notification obligations.

## 11. Your Rights

To exercise rights, contact [privacy@newsfork.com](mailto:privacy@newsfork.com). We aim to respond within 30 days.
