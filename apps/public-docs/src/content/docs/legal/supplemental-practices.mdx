## 1. Purpose

This document supplements the [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service/) and [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy/). It summarizes how **optional automation and AI-assisted features** may rely on **subprocessors**, and the **oversight** expectations when those features are enabled for your organization.

## 2. Subprocessors and configuration

- Assisted features (semantic search, RAG Q&A, News-V scoring enrichment, MCP agent tools) may call **third-party services** under agreements that include confidentiality and data-protection terms appropriate for business SaaS.
- **Configuration and enablement** are controlled by your plan, contract, and administrator settings.
- A current list of subprocessors is available upon request or in our [Data Processing Agreement](/legal/data-processing-agreement/).

## 3. Human review

Users should **review** outputs from assisted features before relying on them for material decisions, consistent with the Terms of Service (Section 8).

AI-generated summaries, classifications, and search results may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for a particular purpose. Do not rely solely on assisted outputs for compliance, legal, financial, or safety-critical decisions.

## 4. Data usage

- Customer Data processed by assisted features is handled per our Privacy Policy and DPA.
- We do not use Customer Data to train third-party foundation models without the customer's explicit consent.
- Logs reference artifact paths only; full prompts and article text are not retained in operational logs beyond what is necessary for support and security.
