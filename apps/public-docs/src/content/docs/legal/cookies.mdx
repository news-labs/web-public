## Overview

Newsfork uses cookies and similar technologies only where necessary to operate our websites and API dashboard.

## Cookies we use

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Session | Authentication and session management | Session |
| Theme preference | Remember light/dark mode on docs and marketing sites | 1 year |

## Cookies we do not use

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or cross-site profiling cookies on our public websites.

## Analytics

If we use analytics, data is aggregated and anonymised. We do not build individual user profiles from website visits.

## Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential session cookies may prevent you from signing in to the API dashboard.

Questions about cookies? Email [privacy@newsfork.com](mailto:privacy@newsfork.com).
