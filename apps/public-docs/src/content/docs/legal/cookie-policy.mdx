## 2. What Are Cookies?

**Cookies** are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website.

**Similar technologies** include local storage, session storage, pixels/web beacons, and SDKs.

## 4. Types of Cookies We Use

### 4.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential and cannot be disabled without affecting functionality.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Session cookie | User session management | Session | Newsfork |
| CSRF token cookie | Security (CSRF protection) | Session | Newsfork |
| Authentication cookie | Authentication state | 7 days | Newsfork |
| cf_clearance | Security (bot protection) | 1 year | Cloudflare |
| __cf_bm | Bot management | 30 minutes | Cloudflare |

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest / Contractual necessity.

### 4.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our websites.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| _ga | Google Analytics — user identification | 2 years | Google |
| _ga_* | Google Analytics — session tracking | 2 years | Google |

**Legal basis:** Consent (we ask before setting analytics cookies).

## 6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner allows you to:

- Accept all cookies
- Accept necessary cookies only
- Customize preferences

Change preferences anytime via **Cookie settings** in the website footer on `www.newsfork.com`.

### 6.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent the site or platform from functioning correctly.

### 6.3 Opt-Out Tools

- [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### 6.4 Consequences of Disabling Cookies

- **Strictly necessary:** Site or platform may not function correctly.
- **Analytics:** We cannot understand usage patterns to improve services.
- **Preference:** Settings (theme, language) will not be remembered.

## 7. Do Not Track Signals

We do not currently respond to Do Not Track signals. Manage cookies as described in Section 6.

## 8. Cookies and the Newsfork Platform
